The January transfer window has been busy, with several big-money moves and significant squad changes across Europe's top leagues.

**Major Moves**
- Jude Bellingham to Real Madrid (€120M)
- Florian Wirtz to Bayern Munich (€100M)
- Victor Boniface to Arsenal (€90M)

Some clubs were active, while others were content to stand pat. The financial realities of modern football mean that only the wealthiest can compete for the best players.

Mid-table clubs struggled to make moves due to budget constraints. This has led to a concentration of talent at the top clubs.

The impact of these transfers will be felt throughout the season. Clubs that strengthened their squads have a better chance of competing for honors.
