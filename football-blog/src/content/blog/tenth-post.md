Major League Soccer's growth in the United States shows no signs of slowing down. With new teams joining and existing ones expanding, the league is experiencing unprecedented growth.

The addition of new markets like San Diego, Portland, and Atlanta has brought the league to more fans than ever before. Attendance figures are at record highs.

The quality of play has improved significantly. With players from around the world joining MLS, the league is becoming more competitive.

The involvement of star players like Lionel Messi and Zlatan Ibrahimović has generated massive interest. Their presence has attracted casual fans to the sport.

**Notable Developments**
- 30 teams planned by 2030
- New stadium construction
- Increased TV rights deals
- Better player development
