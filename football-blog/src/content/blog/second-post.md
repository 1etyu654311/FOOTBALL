Bayer Leverkusen's historic season continues as they extend their unbeaten run to an incredible 30 matches across all competitions. Under the guidance of Xabi Alonso, this German club has defied all expectations.

What makes this achievement even more remarkable is that they remain in contention for all four trophies they're eligible for. The Bundesliga title, DFB-Pokal, and their debut in the Champions League have all kept fans dreaming.

Alonso's tactical approach has been the cornerstone of their success. His ability to adapt formations mid-game and make substitutions that change the game has been exceptional.

Key players like Florian Wirtz and Victor Boniface have been in scintillating form. Their partnership upfront has torn defenses apart, while the defensive unit has been rock solid.

The club's recruitment strategy has also been brilliant. Bringing in players who fit the system rather than big names has created a cohesive unit that plays as one.

As the season progresses, expectations are rising. Can they maintain this incredible form until the very end?
