The Champions League quarter-final draw has produced some mouth-watering matchups. The round of 16 provided plenty of drama, and the quarter-finals promise even more excitement.

**Key Matchups**
- Real Madrid vs Bayern Munich
- Manchester City vs Arsenal
- Inter Milan vs Barcelona
- Liverpool vs Atletico Madrid

These fixtures represent some of the biggest rivalries in European football. The history, the passion, and the stakes are all incredibly high.

Real Madrid and Bayern Munich face off for the 10th time in European competition. Both teams have won the competition 14 times, making this a clash of titans.

In the other tie, Manchester City and Arsenal have a modern rivalry that's gaining momentum. The English clash will be a tactical battle between two of Europe's best managers.

**Previous Encounters**
- Madrid vs Bayern: 6-6 in all competitions
- City vs Arsenal: 3-3 in European meetings
- Inter vs Barcelona: 5-5 in Champions League history
