England's women's national team continues to build on their remarkable World Cup success. The Lionesses have established themselves as a force to be reckoned with on the international stage.

Under manager Sarina Wiegman, the team has developed a distinctive style of play. Their possession-based approach, combined with quick transitions, has proven difficult for opponents to defend against.

Key players like Ella Toone, Beth Mead, and Alessia Russo have been instrumental in their success. Their creativity and finishing have consistently delivered in crucial moments.

The team's depth is also impressive. With several players capable of starting for top European clubs, England has options that many other nations envy.

As they prepare for the next major tournaments, expectations are high. Can they maintain their upward trajectory and potentially win another major trophy?
