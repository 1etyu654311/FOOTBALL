Real Madrid continues to chase Champions League glory under Carlo Ancelotti. The Spanish giants are determined to add another European trophy to their already impressive collection.

Vinicius Junior has been in exceptional form, providing the creativity and pace that has made him one of the most feared wingers in world football. His partnership with Jude Bellingham has been deadly.

The defense has been solid, with Antonio Rudiger anchoring the backline. His leadership and experience have been crucial in important matches.

Ancelotti's ability to rotate his squad without losing quality has been a key factor. Players like Rodrygo and Dani Ceballos have stepped up when needed.

With the La Liga title also in contention, Real Madrid faces a busy schedule. Can they balance domestic and European commitments successfully?
