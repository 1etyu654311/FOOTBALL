Paris Saint-Germain financial backing continues to shape French football. With unlimited budget for transfers, PSG can attract the world best players.

This season the squad depth has been exceptional. From the starting XI to the bench, every player is capable of starting for any other club in Ligue 1.

Kylian Mbappe future remains the biggest question mark. His contract situation has been the subject of intense speculation, with clubs across Europe monitoring the situation closely.

The emergence of young talents like Warren Zaire-Emery and Rayan Cherki has added to PSG appeal. The club is building for the future while maintaining its competitive edge.

In domestic competition, PSG has been dominant, though they occasionally been frustrated in cup competitions. The French Cup and Champions League remain their targets.
